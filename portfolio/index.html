My Story

Hello, my name is Damodharan, and I am a mechanical engineering student at St. Joseph's College of Engineering in Chennai. Before pursuing my BE, I completed my diploma in mechanical engineering.


During my time as a Quality Inspector at Hamilton Houseware Pvt. Ltd, I gained practical experience in ensuring product compliance with specific requirements and maintaining superior quality standards.


In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively contribute to two initiatives aimed at empowering students and working professionals to enhance their skills and advance their careers. Through the GIT Students Career Club and Krishna Project Centre, I provide valuable resources and create opportunities for growth and development.


In my quest for personal and professional growth, I constantly seek out new challenges and opportunities. This has led me to participate in numerous competitions, symposiums, and hackathons held at various colleges and locations. I take great pride in my accomplishments, having secured victories in multiple events, which have helped me cultivate a diverse skill set and expand my knowledge.


Web development stands out as a core area of interest and expertise for me. Proficient in several programming languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, C, and React, I have successfully created over twelve websites that serve as a testament to my skills and passions.


Moreover, I am particularly proud of my mechanical projects. For instance, the "Seed Sowing Vehicle" demonstrates my ability to apply mechanical engineering principles to real-world problems. Another noteworthy project of mine is the "Tri-Wheel Stair Climber," designed to facilitate safe transportation of items on stairs, minimizing the risk of accidents. These projects reflect my dedication to developing innovative solutions for practical challenges.


I am excited about the future and eager to take on new endeavors that allow me to further contribute to my field. Thank you for taking the time to learn about my journey and accomplishments. If you have any questions or would like to connect, please feel free to reach out.
